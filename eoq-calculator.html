WA Method (Main Roads WA):
Hourly volume → vehicles/min → vehicles stopped → queue length by vehicle type
Each vehicle includes a 3 m inter-vehicle gap.   Car: 5.5 m  |  Truck/Bus: 19 m  |  Road Train / B-Double: 36.5 m
In the direction of travel at time of works
Include stop time + clearance time
Vehicle Composition (%)
5.5 m + 3 m gap = 8.5 m
19 m + 3 m gap = 22 m
36.5 m + 3 m gap = 39.5 m
Vehicle percentages must add up to 100%.
NSW Method (Transport for NSW TM):
Hourly volume → vehicles/min → vehicles stopped → queue by vehicle type.
Sight distance: 2D for speeds > 65 km/h  |  1.5D for speeds ≤ 65 km/h. D = posted speed in m (for speeds > 65 km/h) per AS1742.3.
Vehicle Composition (%)
5.5 m + 3 m gap = 8.5 m
19 m + 3 m gap = 22 m
Vehicle percentages must add up to 100%.
QLD Formula (Table 4.3 — TMR Guide):
(Average vehicles × Ma) + (Heavy vehicles × Mo) = Queue length (m)
Minimum: round up to at least 1 average vehicle + 1 heavy vehicle.
From AADT with hourly breakdown:
5-min estimate = Peak hourly ÷ 12 ÷ 2 (two-way road)
For the approach direction being controlled
From AADT only:
5-min estimate = AADT ÷ 10 ÷ 12 ÷ 2 (two-way road)
Total: stop time + clearance + travel through site
Prior to any roadwork speed reduction
